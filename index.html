Earthship planning and construction

Jordskarve is the first of potentially more Earthships that is planned for construction in Liberstad.

Constributing

Any volunteering and contribution to the planning and construction is greatly appreciated. There is a lot of work that goes into both the planning phase, and obviously the construction phase of an Earthship. Please go to the Github repository and the Wiki to help constribute on the planning, and more details about volunteering for construction help will come in the future.
